Procedure



IIIT Dharwad welcomes students from across the country, with diverse backgrounds and far-ranging talents and interests. Our Institute offers admission in B.Tech programmes through JEE Main entrance exam.
We Offer admissions into B.Tech. programmes : Data Science And Artificial Intelligence ( DSAI ) Computer Science Engineering ( CSE ) Electronics And Communication Engineering ( ECE ) at IIIT Dharwad addmissions are through JEE (Mains) Examination process.
Admission to all these programmes offered by the institute are made through a Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) and Central Seat Allocation Board (CSAB) set up by Ministry of Education (MoE) to manage and regulate joint seat allocation for admissions to all government funded technical institutes. There is no other channel of admission allowed.

The minimum academic qualification for admission through JEE (Mains) Examination is passing final examination of 10+2 (Class XII) or its equivalent. Any additional requirement or entry criteria (e.g. minimum percentages or maximum attempts) may be imposed by examination bodies. Currently, foreign students are not admitted, only Indian nationals are admitted at the institute. After seat allocation from JoSAA or CSAB through different rounds, student report at the institute during July-August reporting period every year and register for the first semester to start their academic journey. These students spend some of their formative years in the campus to gain knowledge and develop positive attributes to help the country and the society, by and large.

PREREQUISITES


At the time of institute reporting for the admission, a student has to bring the following list of original documents.
  • EWS Certificate (if applicable) as per JoSAA format
  • Provisional Seat Allotment Letter issued by CSAB/JoSAA
  • JEE Main Score Card
  • JEE Main Admit Card
  • Proof of Date of Birth (10th Certificate and Mark Sheet)
  • Qualifying Examination (12th Certificate and Mark Sheet)
  • Character Certificate from the last School/College attended
  • Transfer Certificate from the last School/College attended
  • Migration Certificate from the last School/College attended
  • Medical Certificate as per JoSAA format
  • Caste Certificate (SC/ST/OBC-NCL) as per JoSAA format
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Certificate for Person with Disabilities (PwD)
  • Aadhar Card (Mandatory)
  • ID Proof (Any govt. issued ID card with photo)
  • Gap Certificate (if applicable)
  • JoSAA Fee Payment Receipt
  • Four Recent Passport Size Photographs CSAB/JoSAA
